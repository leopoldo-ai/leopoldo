# Leopoldo for Legal

Comprehensive legal expertise for Claude: 8 practice areas covering contract law, corporate, disputes, IP, labour, real estate, compliance, and legal operations.

## What you can do

| Area | Capabilities |
|------|-------------|
| Contract Law | Review NDAs, SPAs, subscription agreements; flag red flags; suggest negotiation points |
| Corporate Law | Governance structures, M&A legal, shareholder agreements, board resolutions |
| Dispute Resolution | Case strategy, settlement analysis, litigation risk assessment, arbitration |
| IP and Technology | Patent strategy, licensing, data protection (GDPR), software agreements |
| Labour Law | Employment contracts, termination, restructuring, collective bargaining |
| Real Estate | Purchase agreements, lease review, due diligence, zoning compliance |
| Legal Operations | Matter management, outside counsel, legal spend optimization |
| Compliance | Regulatory mapping, policy drafting, audit preparation, risk registers |
